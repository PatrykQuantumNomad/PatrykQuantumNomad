   - Every node has technicalDescription of at least 90 words
   - No technicalDescription exceeds 200 words
   - technicalDescription differs from simpleDescription (they should NOT be rephrased copies)

4. `describe('content quality - whyItMatters')`:
   - Every node has a non-empty whyItMatters
   - whyItMatters is concise (under 60 words)

5. `describe('cluster coverage')`:
   - Nodes exist for clusters: ai, ml, nn, dl, genai, levels, agentic, devtools
   - Each cluster has at least 1 node
